Our Purpose: The Zero-to-One Era

Majid Hameed
Founder, Fictrpreneur

For years, we’ve seen bright minds with incredible ideas but no access to capital, mentors, or guidance. They don’t fail because they lack ability — they fail because the ecosystem wasn’t built for them.

The answer kept coming back to belief.

Belief — the quiet force that separates those who start from those who only dream. In small rooms, rural towns, and quiet corners, people are waiting to begin. They don’t need luck — they need direction.

Until opportunity became accessible.

At Fictrpreneur, we’re turning belief into structure. We are building a system for the underdogs — for those who are ready to start but have no door to knock on. Because opportunity shouldn’t depend on privilege.

Mentorship & Guidance

Real advice from founders who’ve built from zero — practical, not theoretical.

Seed Capital

Small-ticket capital of ₹40,000–₹50,000 for early validation — because first funding changes everything.

Community

A tribe of doers who hold each other accountable, share resources, and grow together.

We choose to build that structure.

Fictrpreneur is where they start.
